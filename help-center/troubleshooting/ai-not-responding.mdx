---
title: AI Not Responding in Warstellar
description: Fix Warstellar when the AI stops responding to prompts.
---

## Symptoms

- AI does not generate code.
- Prompts appear stuck or timeout.

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection">
    ````bash
    ping api.cursor.so
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload Extension">
    Disable, then re-enable the Warstellar extension in your IDE's extensions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart IDE">
    Close and restart your IDE to reset the connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

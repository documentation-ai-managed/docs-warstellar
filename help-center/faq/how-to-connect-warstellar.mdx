## Overview

Warstellar integrates with major IDEs like VS Code.

## Steps

1. Install the "Cursor: Warstellar" extension via your IDE's marketplace.
2. Open the Command Palette and run `Warstellar: Sign In`.
3. Enter your Cursor account credentials.

## Confirmation

You should see "Warstellar Connected" in your IDE's status bar.

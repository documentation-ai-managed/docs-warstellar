## Overview

Warstellar can suggest code as you type using AI.

## How to Enable

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Warstellar Settings">
    In your IDE, go to Settings > Extensions > Warstellar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Auto-Complete">
    Enable "AI Inline Auto-Completion" from the options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Coding">
    Begin typing in any fileâ€”suggestions will appear automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
